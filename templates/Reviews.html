Favourites

Such a cute show. I truly fealt like comforted while watching this show. I have been going through some things recently but watching this show made me so happy and it just distracts me and helps me not think about everything. I also love most of the music soundtrack and love how wholesome some of the characters are. I would’ve never thought I would enjoy something like this especially since I have never watched figure skating or anime. It’s an amazing show and I could watch it 10 more times and still not get bored of it.

Boku no Hero Academia is the anime that got me into anime. I love the plot very much. The idea of a super-human society and heroes is so fascinating! I wish I had a quirk too, I wish I could become a hero too. This show made me believe that power or not, I can be the one too. It is a full-pack show with awesomeness! Must Watch for all Rookies.

Jujutsu kaisen is the year's best anime. The Screenplay keeps you engagaged in each and every episode and a well executed adaptated manga. Pacing, Execution, Direction is pitch perfect and will make you crave for more and more. Animation scenes and Art is amazingly handled by MAPPA Studio.

Jujutsu Kaisen

"Hashitte koronde Kienai itami daite wa Sekai ga matteru Kono isshun wo"

Greatest TV show of all time, the manga is incredible and I can’t wait for Season 4B. Eren Yeager is probably one of the greatest characters in all of anime. Isayama took a big risk with Eren’s character and it paid off. What better way to improve upon an already flawed protagonist by pitting him against his friends. I don’t want to call Eren an antagonist either, his motives are very understandable and he is so well written. I hope Season 4B of the anime is filled with the same amount of thrills as the manga gave me.

Naruto is definitely worth watching! There are so many life lessons that you would learn while watching the show that no school teaches you. Naruto is truly one of a kind, it's just a bit too long, but all the episodes are worth the HYPE!

Tokyo Revengers

This show is gonna be a leading one in shonen for the next four years. Truly enjoyed it and loved watching it! definitely gonna start reading the manga.

To Your Eterenity

It is a great slow burning show, the characters are unique and awesome. They have their own story and are well developed. The animation steals the show to a great extend and all these things together makes the show a total piece of awesomeness!

The Rising of the Shield Hero

I cant say anything more except this anime is PERFECTION and a MASTERPIECE !! Great Plot, Great Character development.

Assassination Classroom

Assassination Classroom is a beautiful intricate storyline with real depth and I thoroughly enjoyed it. Sadly a large chunk of season 2 feels like filler and that really damaged it but it returned to form in it's final few episodes all leading the incredible finale.

5 centimeter per Second

If you like beautiful art and animation, plus a good story, then "5 Centimeters Per Second" is a must-see. Even though it is only one hour long, the movie talks about heart-braking situations and hidden feelings, things we can all relate to.

A Whisker Away

I expected no more than a cheesy drama but I was so wrong, i totally related with the girl and the lesson taught was actually pretty human (talking about a movie with a cat huh? Lol), it's beautiful not only with the plot but visually stunning it certainly is, give it a chance, it's totally worth it.

I want to eat your Pancreas

I really enjoyed the movie its emotional definitely but its also heartwarming and wholesome at the same time. Overall a must watch.

Garden of Words

Overall, Garden of words is a really good piece of art. It's definitely filled with Makoto's signature style and a definite recommendation to watch.

Given

A very chill light weight music based anime. It was a beautiful journey.
